DRESS STANDARDS

Appropriate dress is modest, neat, clean, and avoids extremes.

INAPPROPRIATE DRESS

Shorts of any length, PE type clothing, sweat suits, hats of any type, bandanas, and any printed message or picture that is contrary to gospel principles. Youth will refrain from wearing the following:

Young Women
  • Skirts above the knee (Incl. slits)
  • Sleeveless shirts or blouses, tank tops or any clothing showing a bare midriff
  • Clothing that is revealing, backless or low-cut
  • Leggings may be worn, but will be treated as nylons, and can only be worn under knee-length skirts
  • Inappropriate piercings, more than one pair of earrings
  • “Yoga” pants or jeans with holes
Young Men
  • Tank tops
  • Clothing that is patched, frayed, torn, holes or sagging
  • Earrings are inappropriate for young men, as are other body piercings
